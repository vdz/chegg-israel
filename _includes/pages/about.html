Who we are

Chegg Israel team was built on a foundation of a 3D3R family — a group of close friends and fierce professionals. After 3D3R has joined Chegg in its mission to evolve higher education, the newly formed Chegg Middle East office began to expand.

How do you expand a family? Well, it’s a laborious process that requires both handpicking new members and solid definition if family values. The outcome is a vibrant and colourful pack of top professional, who care about what they came here to do, and … for each other.

We are family

We love the Israeli intimacy. We set out to build an intimate and warm work place. A family. We are close here at Chegg Israel. We like out people a lot.

We even share the subject we love and care about in weekly talks, when each team member talks about a subject dear to her heart. From surfing, through photo journalism to duck hunting and beyond. So obviously we love coming to the office. Sharing out highs and lows with people who understand us.

Culture

Our initial goal was to create a fusion between the best of Israeli culture and the grand product vision of the Silicone Valley. It’s not enough to be only a great engineer — you also have to be able to navigate in the multinational landscape of Chegg’s international operation. We invest in our cultural development, so that we can work with our colleagues in Ukraine for example, and actually understand their culture.

What is our culture? We are geeks, gamers, lovers of books and music, art, sports and travel. We enjoy good food, good conversation. We love sharing. We volunteer because it’s important to us. We do the reserves because it’s an obligation. We sponsor events that promote otherwise neglected issues.

In the end we want to be able to bring our kids to work, and let them experience us crafting …
We — at Chegg — made it our business to tip the balance in favour of fair and rewarding education, with great outcomes, promising and attainable careers and self fulfilment. We’re making this dream come true by building great products that help students save time, save money and get smarter.
